CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra Y Magsalin
  • Ibarra is a young, wealthy mestizo who has recently returned to the
    Philippines from seven years of study in Europe.

  • He is smart, well-respected and very idealistic.
Maria Clara
  • She is a woman of high social status and is believed to be the
    goddaughter of Father Damaso, as well as as the daughter of Captain
    Tiago.

  • She is truly Father Damaso's biological child, as a result of their
    scandalous relationship with Captain Tiago's wife.
Father Damaso
  • Father Damaso is an elderly, ruthless corrupt and a Spanish priest who
    has spent almost two decades residing among the native Filipinos.

  • He has spent all that time among them, but the years have done nothing to
    endear him or give him any sympathy for his "flock."

  • He is profoundly prejudiced, spiteful, vengeful and he has no problem using
    his immense influence to destroy people who have wronged him.
Captain Tiago
  • Captain Tiago is a rich Filipino bussiness man and one of the powerful influencers in San Diego.
    He is the biological father of Maria Clara, husband of Pia Alba and cousin of Aunt Isabel.
    He's also one of the closest friends of Father Damaso and Mr. Rafael Ibarra.

  • He is brown-skinned, short, chubby, old, religious, meek, well-respected and a submissive man.

Basilio
